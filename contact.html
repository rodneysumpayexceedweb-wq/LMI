OUR FACILITIES

LMI has three main operating facilities that are strategically located in California and Nevada to service it's ever growing list of Builder partners.

Each facility is distinctively set-up to fabricate LMI builder products and to support its highly trained installation crews. In addition, each location has a dedicated sales, scheduling, measuring and customer service department in order to better serve it's builder partners.
