WARDROBE DOORS

PRODUCTS

LMI wardrobe doors combine refined design with unmatched durability, bringing both style and functionality to every space. From sleek steel and aluminum-framed panels to customizable finishes and configurations, our doors are crafted to complement modern interiors while maximizing usability.

Built with premium materials, they deliver a smooth, effortless operation, long-lasting performance, and a clean aesthetic that enhances any room. Whether for residential or commercial projects, LMI wardrobe doors offer a seamless blend of practicality and lasting quality.

ALUMINUM BIPASS BOTTOM ROLLED WARDROBE DOORS

8000 Series

The 8000 series aluminum framed wardrobe door perfectly combines functionality and contemporary design. This versatile system can be mounted under a header or directly to the ceiling, ensuring that it fits seamlessly into any space. Featuring concealed bottom rollers, it easily adjusts to accommodate out-of-square openings.

This wardrobe door system uses ball-bearing twin wheel top guides for effortless operation, and the ball-bearing bottom rollers with internal gears allow for a maximum height adjustment of 7/8 inch. The 8000 series closet door is ideal for those who demand style and performance.
