Aluminum Framed Wardrobe Doors

7000 Series

The 7000 series aluminum framed wardrobe door perfectly combines functionality and contemporary design. This versatile system can be mounted under a header or directly to the ceiling, ensuring that it fits seamlessly into any space. Featuring concealed bottom rollers, it easily adjusts to accommodate out-of-square openings.

This wardrobe door system uses ball-bearing twin wheel top guides for effortless operation, and the ball-bearing bottom rollers with internal gears allow for a maximum height adjustment of 7/8 inch. The 7000 series closet door is ideal for those who demand style and performance.
