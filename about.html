ABOUT US

OVER 50 YEARS IN THE BUILDING INDUSTRY

For over five decades, LMI has been the benchmark in fabricating and installing specialty bath and closet products. Founded in 1972 and headquartered in Ontario, California, we cater to the unique needs of residential builders, commercial high-rise developers, general contractors, retailers, and RV manufacturers across California and Nevada.

Our extensive product line, including shower enclosures, wardrobe doors, vanity and wall mirrors, closet organizers, and shelving, is renowned for its precision and ability to meet the exacting standards of today's construction industry.
