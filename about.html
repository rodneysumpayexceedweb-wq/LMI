ABOUT US

OVER 50 YEARS SERVING THE BUILDING INDUSTRY

For more than 50 years, Larry Methvin Installation (LMI) has set the standard for quality fabrication and installation of specialty shower enclosures and closet products throughout California and Nevada.

Established in 1972 and headquartered in Ontario, California, LMI has built a reputation for excellence by serving the diverse needs of residential builders, commercial high-rise developers, general contractors, retailers, and RV manufacturers.

Our product portfolio spans a wide range of applicationsâ€”including custom shower enclosures, wardrobe doors, vanity and wall mirrors, closet organizers, and shelving systems. Each product is crafted with precision and backed by a commitment to performance, durability, and design flexibility.

With decades of proven expertise, LMI continues to be the trusted partner for construction professionals who demand dependable service, consistent quality, and innovative solutions tailored to the evolving standards of the industry.

At LMI, our unwavering commitment to quality and innovation is matched only by our dedication to customer service. With four manufacturing and distribution centers in California and Nevada, we maintain strict control over every supply chain step, ensuring that our clients receive the best possible products and service.

Our operational excellence is the cornerstone of our ability to meet the unique challenges faced by our clients. This enables us to deliver premium products and tailored solutions. Our technical expertise and dedication to continuous improvement have made us a preferred partner for leading builders and contractors in California and Nevada.

Our mission is not just to meet expectations, but to consistently exceed them.
We strive to transform challenges into opportunities, delivering exceptional results
with every project.
